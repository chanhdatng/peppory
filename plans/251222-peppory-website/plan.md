## Key Design Decisions

### Color Palette (Wabi-sabi)
- Primary: Terracotta `#C4A484`
- Secondary: Sage `#9CAF88`
- Neutral: Sand `#E5DDD3`
- Dark: Charcoal `#4A4A4A`
- Light: Cream `#FAF8F5`

### Typography
- Heading: Playfair Display (serif, elegant)
- Body: Source Sans 3 (readable, clean)
- Accent: Caveat (handwritten, organic)

### Texture
- Subtle paper grain overlay
- Organic curved shapes
- Generous whitespace
